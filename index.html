Our Philosophy

Coffee is not
a beverage.
It is art.

Etherix was born from an obsession with the extraordinary — the rare, the wild, the poetic. We travel to altitudes above 1800 meters to source beans from farmers who treat their crop as a calling. Every harvest is traced, every roast is intentional.

We believe the best coffee doesn't announce itself. It asks you to slow down, to breathe, to notice the notes of dark cherry, the lingering warmth, the quiet complexity of something truly alive.

The Journey

FARM TO
Cup

01

The Farm

High-altitude farms, mist-covered mornings, and generations of knowledge. We partner directly with smallholder farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala.

02

The Harvest

Selectively hand-picked at peak ripeness. Only the reddest, heaviest cherries make the cut — a standard that yields barely 10% of the crop.

03

The Roast

Small-batch roasted in our atelier. Temperature curves tuned to each bean's unique density. Never rushed. Never compromised.

04

Your Cup

From our roastery to your hands within 72 hours of roasting. Every bag sealed with nitrogen — preserving peak aromatics and the living essence of the bean.

The Founder
"I didn't want to open a café. I wanted to build a sanctuary — a place where the cup holds more than coffee."

Etherix began in 2019 in a 200 sq ft roastery in Bandra, Mumbai. Arjun Mehta, then a disillusioned financial analyst, had just returned from a year traveling through Ethiopia's coffee highlands — a journey that changed everything.

He saw farmers rise before dawn, hand-sort cherries by lamplight, and pour their lives into a crop that was sold for almost nothing. He came home obsessed — not just with the flavors, but with the people behind them. Etherix was built to honor both.

Today, we source from 14 origins, pay an average of 3× the fair trade premium, and fund schooling for children on three of our partner farms. Every cup you drink is a direct investment in someone's story.

Chaitanya Patil Founder & Head Roaster · Etherix Café
Our Commitment

EARTH
First

Direct Trade

We bypass commodity markets entirely. Every farmer receives our price — not the market's. We visit every farm we source from, at least twice a year.

Zero Plastic

Our bags are 100% compostable — plant-based liners, water-based inks, paper labels. Even the valve is biodegradable. No plastic. None.

Carbon Neutral

Our roastery runs on solar. Shipping is offset through verified forestry projects in the Western Ghats. We publish our carbon data quarterly.

Water Stewardship

We work only with wet mills that have proper wastewater treatment. Processing water is recycled on-site — protecting rivers downstream.

Farmer Welfare

We fund crop insurance, medical access, and school scholarships for children of our partner farmers. Good coffee comes from good lives.

Agroforestry

We source only shade-grown coffee alongside native trees — protecting biodiversity, sequestering carbon, and producing measurably better flavor.
